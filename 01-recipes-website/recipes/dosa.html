Dosa Recipe

What is this 'Dosa'?

Dosa is the ever-popular South Indian breakfast of crispy crepes made with ground rice and lentil batter that is fermented. With just a handful of ingredients learn to make the classic Dosa recipe from scratch

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup Parboiled Rice (idli-dosa rice)
  • 3/4 cup Regular Rice
  • 1/2 cup Whole Urad Dal (without skin) or Split Urad Dal (black lentils)
  • 1/4 teaspoon Fenugreek Seeds (methi dana)
  • 1/2 tablespoon Chana Dal (gram lentils), optional
  • Water, as needed
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil, for shallow frying
